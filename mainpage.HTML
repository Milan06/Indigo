HackIt Coding

Enjoy the interactive games we have here!

Pong

Pong is an interactive two player game. The objective of this is to score by getting the ball to maneuver into the goal while preventing your opponent from scoring. The two players battle it out and the first one to score 7 times wins. Challenge your friend and see who comes out on top!


Float

Float is an endless interactive single player game. The objective is to avoid all the obstacles and get the highest score possible. Challenge your friend to see who can get the highest score!

Breakout

Breakout is an interactive single player game. In Breakout, there is a layer of bricks that the player is trying to break to gain more points. You will be given 3 lives to try and knock down all of the bricks. Have fun breaking the bricks!

Hangman

The objective of Hangman is for the player of the game to guess the name of different coding languages. The player will get 9 guesses each round to correctly guess the language. Have fun guessing!

Rock Paper Scissors

Although this game is much simpler than the other games on this site, it is a lot of fun! The player gets to choose rock, paper, or scissors and then will go against the random generator in the computer to see who comes out on top. Ready for "Rock, Paper, Scissor says Shoot?"

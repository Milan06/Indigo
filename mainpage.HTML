Enjoy the interactive games we have here!


Pong

Pong is an interactive two player game. The objective of this is to score by getting the ball to manuever into the goal while preventing your opponent from scoring. The two players battle it out and the first one to score 7 times wins. Challenge your friend and see who comes out on top!


Flappy Bird

Flappy Bird is an endless interactive single player game. The objective is to avoid all the obstacles and get the highest score possible. Challenge your friend to see who can get the highest score!
